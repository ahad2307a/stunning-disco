F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is nail art?

    Nail art is the creative practice of decorating and embellishing nails with various designs and techniques (Nail art is the creative application of different colors, textures, and designs on fingernails or toenails to enhance their appearance. It is a form of self-expression and a popular way of adding a personal touch to one's style. Nail art can include various techniques, such as freehand painting, stamping, water marbling, and 3D decorations, among others. It has become a popular trend in recent years, with many nail artists showcasing their skills on social media platforms)

  • Which nail art look classy?

    There are many nail art designs that can look classy, depending on personal style and preference. Some classic designs that are often considered classy include French tips, nude or neutral colors, and simple geometric shapes. Elegant and minimalist designs with negative space or delicate patterns can also give a classy look.

  • What is a French tip nail art design?

    A French tip design is a classic style where the tips of the nails are painted white or another color, while the rest of the nail is left natural or painted a different color

  • What is a Pedicure?

    A pedicure is a beauty treatment for the feet that involves cleaning, grooming, and beautifying the toenails and skin on the feet. During pedicures, dead skin cells are exfoliated from the feet. The feet are then treated with a nourishing lotion and the toenails are cut, shaped, and polished. Pedicures are a type of self-care that can help with relaxation and stress reduction while also enhancing the look and health of the feet.

  • HOW Long does a pedicure take to dry?

    While a pedicure may feel dry to the touch an hour or two after the pedicure treatment has finished, that doesnâ€™t mean that the pedicure has actually finished drying. Pedicures can take between 12 and 24 hours to fully dry. This means that you should wait at least 12 hours before getting into a hot shower or bath with a new pedicure. Bathing any sooner than this can risk lifting or warping the aesthetic of the pedicure you just got.

  • What is a manicure?

    The classic manicure is a nail care method that involves treating the hand and nails. The treatment is performed by an artificial nail artist. During the treatment the nails are disinfected and then soaked. This is followed by filling the shape and length to the liking of the guest, grooming the skin around the nail, removing the dead skin, pressing up the cuticle, massaging the hand. Finally, polish the nails, apply skin care creams and apply nail care. Manicure can even include hand scrubs or hand wraps.

  • what is nail extension?

    Nail extension are one of the most famous ways to get longer, stronger and more attractive nails. People love to get unique styles and colourful colours on their extensions. Nail care is something that most people don't focus on, but it is surely a major part of grooming and presentation. Nail extension is a trick to get the best out of nail art and polish. The technique is to use gel or acrylic nails to extend your natural nail. However, this comes with a range of benefits as well as side effects. You can make your nails look better.
